DescriptionThe fourth-born of the four Battle Chatelaine sisters, Nita speaks in a unique way and is very energetic. In the Battle Maison, she is in charge of Single Battles.

Nita (Japanese: ラニュイ Lanuit) is a Pokémon Trainer in Pokémon Masters EX, based on the character of the same name in the core series games. She forms a sync pair with Incarnate Forme Landorus. The sync pair can be obtained by exchanging a BP Sync Pair Super Voucher, and has been available since August 28, 2022.

Trivia

Origin

Nita's design is based on her appearance in Pokémon X, Y, Omega Ruby, and Alpha Sapphire. Landorus is one of the Pokémon that Nita uses during the Super Single Battle against her in the core series games. Landorus's Dex entry comes from Pokémon Black.
