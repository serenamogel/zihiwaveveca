Rauw Alejandro Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, March 28, 2024

What is the relationship of Rauw Alejandro?

Rauw Alejandro at the present is in love with a beautiful and equally talented singer Rosalia. The rumors of their dating started when they were seen holding hands after watching The Nice Guy movie in Los Angeles.

Then his girlfriend made the news official when she posted an IG post in 2022 where she shared a picture of her in Rauw’s arms.

Who is Rauw Alejandro?

Rauw Alejandro is a Puerto Rican national singer and rapper. Rauw Alejandro has launched two albums Afrodisíaco (2020) and Vice Versa (2021) for which he has bagged several awards.

As of this writing, he has altogether 110 nominations plus 24 wins. Premios Tu Música Urbano nominated him the most in 2019 as well as in 2020.

As an actor, he did a brief part in the 2022’s comedy-action movie titled Sky Roja.

Rauw Alejandro- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

His birthdate is 10 Jan 1993 and he was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico as Raúl Alejandro Ocasio Ruiz. The name of his mother is María Nelly Ruiz (backing vocalist) and his father’s identity is Raúl Ocasio (guitarist).

His mom plays an important to his musical career as she made him to listen, Elvis Presley, Michael Jackson, and Chris Brown.

Further, Alejandro’s ancestry is Puerto Rican and as per his academics, he was at the University of Puerto Rico after his high school studies.

Rauw Alejandro- Professional Life, Career

Rauw Alejandro gave his fans two studio albums. His first album came out in 2020 Afrodisíaco, and then Vice Versa in 2021 through labels Sony Latin, and Duars Entertainment. He also has released two EPs Trap Cake, Vol. 1 plus Vol. 2.

The list of his song credits as a lead are,

  • “La Oportunidad”
  • “Olvidemos”
  • “Que Le Dé”
  • “Ponte Pa’ Mí”
  • “Sexo Virtual”
  • “Desesperados”
  • “Gracias por Nada”

He was seen in other artists’ songs such as Secreto with Amarion, Mírame with Nio Garcia and Lenny Tavárez, “Cuerpo en Venta” with Noriel, “Dembow 2020” with Yandel, “Fantasía Sexual” with Myke Towers, “Problemón” with Álvaro Díaz, etc.

How much is the net worth of Rauw Alejandro?

By 2022’s sources, he has an estimated net worth of $15 million. However, his yearly salary is not yet stated.

Rauw Alejandro- Rumors, Controversy

This Reggaetonplus Latin R&B genre singer is keeping himself away from scandals as well as rumors. But after moving to Florida, he went to join the Premier Development League (PDL). Unfortunately, he was not selected which broke him.

This lead him to depression and to overcome this, he shifted his interest from soccer to music.

What are the body features of Rauw Alejandro?

Rauw Alejandro’s height is 5 ft 8 inches and his body weight is 72 kg. On top of that, he has black hair and eyes in dark brown color too.

Social Media

Rauw Alejandro is officially present on social sites such as Instagram with over 14.5 million followers.

His Twitter account is also followed by more than 2M people. He is also active on Facebook, and TikTok with over 130k and 9.8M fans respectively.
