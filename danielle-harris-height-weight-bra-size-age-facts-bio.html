Famous American actress and film director, Danielle Andrea Harris is born on June 1, 1977. Harris is best known as a scream queen for her participation in numerous horror films including four entries in the Halloween series. She received her debut role as an actress at the age of seven as Samantha “Sammi” Garretson in the ABC soap opera One Life to Live. Following her early television work, Danielle successfully auditioned for the role of Jamie Lloyd from the fourth edition of the Halloween franchise. Halloween 4: The Return of Michael Myers was released in 1988 and earned commercial acclaim and success. She reprised her role in next sequels of Halloween and received recognition and success. Along with it, Harris as well took over the lead role of Marybeth Dunston in the Hatchet trilogy, for Hatchet II and Hatchet III. Her other major work in television and films included See No Evil 2, Havenhurst, Roseanne and Holliston.

In addition to that, Danielle is also noted for her voice acting, from 1998 to 2004 she played the role of Debbie Thornberry for the complete TV series run of The Wild Thornberrys and related films Rugrats Go Wild and The Wild Thornberrys Movie. In 2013, she as well made her directorial debut from the horror comedy film Among Friends. As for her personal life, Danielle Harris has been married to David Gross since 2014.
