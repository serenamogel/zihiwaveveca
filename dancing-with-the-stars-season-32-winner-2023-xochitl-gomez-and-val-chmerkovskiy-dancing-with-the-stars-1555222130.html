Explore the exciting story of "Dancing With the Stars" Season 32, where Xochitl Gomez and Val Chmerkovskiy won in a fantastic live competition on ABC and Disney+. See their amazing dance moves, making this season full of moments you won't forget.

Dancing With the Stars Season 32

"Dancing with the Stars" Season 32 started on September 26, 2023, and it was special because it was the first season to be shown live on both ABC and Disney+ at the same time. Alfonso Ribeiro hosted the show, and Julianne Hough, a former dancer and judge, was the co-host. People really liked watching the live broadcasts. The season ended on December 5, 2023, with actress Xochitl Gomez and Val Chmerkovskiy winning and showing off their awesome dance skills.

Dancing With the Stars Season 32 Winner 2023

Xochitl Gomez and Val Chmerkovskiy won "Dancing With the Stars" Season 32 in a big finale with five couples. They got the special Mirrorball trophy on Tuesday night, making it a really important moment for the show. This win is extra special for Chmerkovskiy because it's the third time he's won the Mirrorball trophy. He won before with Rumer Willis in 2015 and Laurie Hernandez in 2016.

Xochitl Gomez and Val Chmerkovskiy

Xochitl Gomez and Val Chmerkovskiy joined forces on the TV dance show "Dancing with the Stars" Season 32 and ended up winning in a really awesome way. The two of them, a 17-year-old actress and a pro dancer, wowed everyone with their amazing skills and exciting performances all season long. Especially cool was their last dance in the big finale, where they danced to the song "Que Calor" by District 78. Gomez, who's proud of her Mexican heritage, and Chmerkovskiy worked really well together, making dances that the judges and the audience loved. Winning the special mirrorball trophy was a huge deal for them, proving they were the best in the competition.
