Markiplier | Career

You have likely heard about Markiplier if you have been around YouTube for some years.

The YouTuber was a well-known content creator roughly five years ago, but with his almost 30.1 million subscribers, he still has a lot of appeal.

Markiplier originally joined YouTube in March 2012, and the following month, he published his debut video.

His first YouTube series was a playthrough of the video game “Amnesia: The Dark Descent.”

Due to some of his previous mishappenings with YouTube thereafter, Markiplier started “Five Nights at Freddy’s” in August 2014.

As a result, it quickly became his most popular playthrough, with over 80 million views.

He then played other series games and took part in a cameo in Freddy’s AR “Five Nights: Special Delivery” in 2019.

Subsequently, in September 2015, Markiplier appeared with YouTuber colleague Jenna Mae on Jimmy Kimmel Live.

Markiplier was named 6th on the ‘Variety’ list of America’s Most Important Celebrities in 2015.
