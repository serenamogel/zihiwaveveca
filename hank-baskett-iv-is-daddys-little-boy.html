HANK BASKETT IV IS DADDYS LITTLE BOY

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, March 15, 2024

Happy Birthday Hank IV! The little one celebrated his fourth birthday on Wednesday, December 11, 2013, with mom and dad overjoyed about their little one’s special day. “4yrs ago today my wife blessed me with the greatest son a father could have! #HappyBirthday Hank IV. Papa loves you,” tweeted Hank Baskett.

The NFL star and his wife, Kendra Wilkinson, recently shared with OK! magazine that they will be having a girl next year. “Hank and I are so happy and content in our lives right now,” said Kendra in the exclusive interview.

The television personality told E! a few weeks ago that little Hank wants a brother for a little sibling. “He wants a baby brother, but we keep telling him it might be a sister and he’s like, ‘No! I don’t want a sister,'” said the celebrity. Kendra also commented on her pregnancy telling media, “I’m actually showing a lot. If I’m wearing tighter clothes, people are like, ‘Are you like four months?’ I’m like, ‘No, I’m still at the end of my first trimester.’ I’m still feeling it, feeling the grossness and the pain.”

Kendra gave birth to Hank IV in 2009.
