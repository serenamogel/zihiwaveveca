Exclusive details of Abhiram Daggubati’s destination wedding

Published on Dec 2, 2023 8:44 pm IST

We had already reported that Abhiram Daggubati, younger son of senior producer Suresh Daggubati and actor Rana Daggubati’s younger brother, will be getting married this December. And here are some exclusive updates on this high-profile wedding.

Abhiram will tie the knot with Prathyusha Chaparala at Anantara Kalutara, a scenic 5-star resort in Sri Lanka. The wedding will take place on December 6 at 8:50 pm. The auspicious occasion will be graced by nearly 200 close friends, family members and celebrities. The guests will be landing in Sri Lanka on Monday to ring in the wedding celebrations.

The 3-day wedding celebrations will kick-start on Monday (Dec 4) with a grand Dinner party at 8:30 pm. On Tuesday, the Mehendi ceremony will be held at 4:30 pm, followed by dinner. And on Wednesday, the Pellikuthuru ceremony will be held at 10:30 am and then the wedding ceremony will begin at 7 pm. The Sumuhurtham is at 8:50 pm.

The Daggubati family will be throwing a grand reception upon their return to Hyderabad. This will be attended by the who’s who of the film, business and political worlds and fans. Stay tuned for further updates on this starry wedding.
