Bunty Agarwal Age & Early Life

Bunty Agarwal was born in 1969, and she is now 53 years old. She is originally from Hyderabad, India. Bunty attended St. George’s Grammer School in Hyderabad during her academic career.

She later earned a diploma in interior design from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, also in Hyderabad. Bunty’s father’s name is Ramavatar Agarwal, and her mother’s name is Bimla Devi. Similarly, she has a sibling brother named Rajiv Agarwal.

Bunty Agarwal Professional Life

In terms of professionalism, Bunty Bajaj works as an interior designer and entrepreneur. In 2018, she and her daughter Miheeka appeared on the cover of the magazine You & I. Bunty is also the director and creative director of the well-known Indian jewelry brand Krsala.
