Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, May 5, 2024

Can you grow chives from cuttings?

Chives don’t grow well from cuttings, but they can be grown from a bulb, similar in appearance to green onions. You are most likely to find chives still attached to the bulb in the organic produce section or your local farmer’s market. Directions: Plant the bulbs in a pot with ample soil.

How do you regrow chives?

Steps
  • Cut chives just above the base of the root system. About an inch.
  • Fill seedling grow pods with good grow soil.
  • Place cut chives in grow soil so the base of the chives are just above the dirt.
  • Water and let grow. Repot once big enough.
