8 Things You Didnt Know About Sasha Agha

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, April 30, 2024

➡ Sasha Agha Biography

Sasha Agha, who is a Pakistani British actress and singer, born on 1st January 1992 and works in Hindi films. She made her Bollywood film debut through ‘Aurangzeb’ which was released in the year 2013. In the film, she appeared as a lead actress in which she worked alongside Aditya Chopra, and unfortunately, her debut film got flopped. Since then, she struggled a lot, gave her best, and appeared in many films, and also established her career in the field of music, and became a great singer. However, the actress has built her career prolific by working on many acting and music projects. Well, to know more about her, let us have a look at these unknown facts we have compiled for you!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Sasha Agha

1- Multi-talented Star

Sasha Agha is a talented girl and is known for being multitalented as she is blessed with superb acting and singing skills. Undoubtedly, she has a melodious, enthralling voice that amuses everyone.

2- She & Salma Agha

Do you guys know who is the stunning actress, Sasha Agha? Let us tell you that Sasha Agha is the daughter of the Pakistani veteran actress, Salma Agha who was famous for her good looks and excellent acting chops at her time.

3- Flopped Film

The actress, Sasha Agha took the start of her career through a Bollywood debut film named ‘Aurangzeb’ which was released in the year 2013, and unfortunately, the film went flop. But she did not lose hope.

4- Comes from an Artistical Background

Sasha Agha comes from an artistical background as her father, Rehman Khan aka Rehmatullah Khan, who was a former Pakistani Squash Player. While her mother Salma Agha is a renowned actress and singer. Her brother Liaqat Ali Khan is a Badminton Player.

5- Hobbies

The actress loves spending time with her hobbies as Sasha Agha loves reading and writing music and poetry. Also, she loves cooking and traveling. Once she revealed that whenever she gets time, she loves spending time with her hobbies.

6- Favorite Actors

Sasha Agha admires her most favorite Bollywood actors including Superstars Salman Khan, Shahrukh Khan, and Kangana Ranaut who are famous for their extremely dedicated professionals.

7- Favorite Movies

Sasha Agha’s most favorite Bollywood films are ‘Mera Naam Hai Joker’ and ‘Queen’ whom she loves watching and never forgets to praise the entire casts of the movies.

8- Other Favorites

The actress loves homemade food. While her favorite colors are Red and Black and these primary colors soothe her. While being a traveler, her favorite destination place is London.
