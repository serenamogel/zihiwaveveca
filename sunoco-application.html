Sunoco Application: Jobs &amp; Careers Online

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, April 10, 2024

Are you thinking of applying for a job at Sunoco?

This prominent company was established more than a hundred years ago and is a leading manufacturer of petroleum and petrochemicals. Sunoco is known for its eco-friendly practices and employs more than 10,000 workers.

There are also more than four thousand Sunoco gas stations scattered across the United States. There are regular vacancies in both corporate areas of the company and the gas stations.

So, let’s take an in-depth look at how to make sure your Sunoco Application: Jobs & Careers Online stands out from the crowd.

Facts About Working At Sunoco

You need to be at least eighteen years old to work at Sunoco. The company’s gas stations and other divisions operate around the clock to provide continuous service. Employees of all types are often required to work a range of shift patterns to ensure continuous service.

Sunoco is committed to training employees and helping them develop new skills. When managerial roles become available, current employees are encouraged to apply for them. This may involve relocating to a different part of the country to head a corporate department or gas station.

Sunoco Job Opportunities

There is a wide range of entry-level positions in Sunoco gas stations, all of which come with paid training. Experienced leaders with specialist skills are also hired to head gas stations and corporate departments. Here are some of the key roles you can apply for at Sunoco and the typical tasks that go with them.

Fuel Attendant

This job mainly involves greeting customers and fueling their vehicles. Fuel attendants need to ensure that the fuel stations are clean and the working environment is safe. They need to greet customers and provide them with a first-rate service.

Sales Associate

These employees work in a Sunoco gas station and assist customers with their purchases. They help customers find the right products for their needs and need to have detailed knowledge of company products. Other duties include stocking shelves and keeping the gas station store clean and tidy.

Cashier

This job revolves around receiving payments from customers for products and operating the cash register. Cashiers need to be able to handle a range of different payment methods and provide the correct change. They need strong customer service skills as well as basic math skills.

Shift Leader

This is a low-level managerial role that involves supervising other employees and motivating them. Shift leaders are responsible for training new employees and making sure they are working efficiently. They also interact with customers and need to be able to step into other roles on demand.

Store Manager

These professionals are charged with overseeing a single Sunoco gas station. They need to make sure that all areas of the gas station are operating smoothly and making a profit. This includes performing inventory and designing new promotional campaigns.

Store managers should constantly be looking for ways of improving the gas station and boosting profits. When needed, they interview and hire new employees and make sure they have the right skills. Other tasks include creating work schedules and payroll and meeting with customers and general managers.

Truck Driver

These professional drivers are charged with safely and efficiently transporting crude oil to pipeline injection stations. It is necessary to test crude oil to make sure it meets the Sunoco quality standards. Truck drivers also maintain their vehicle and make sure it meets Department of Transportation regulations.

It is necessary to hold a special license to be a truck driver for Sunoco. Drivers also undergo regular testing to make sure they meet the company’s safety rules and policies. People who enjoy spending long periods on the open road can excel in this role and make around $100,000 per year.

Accounting Analyst

This role is designed for employees with an accounting degree and involves reporting financial statistics. Accounting analysts need to keep careful records of audits and taxes and resolve customers’ invoicing concerns. This role traditionally takes place in one of Sunoco’s call centers, although some employees work remotely.

Extra Sunoco Employee Benefits

All Sunoco employees receive discounts on company products, tuition assistance, and paid training programs. Full-time employees are provided with a competitive benefits package that includes paid time off and annual vacation days. Other benefits include medical insurance packages including life insurance and access to company retirement plans.

How To Apply?

The careers section of the Sunoco website features separate sections for corporate and retail jobs. Click on the area of the company you want to work in and choose a job opportunity. You can then click to download the Sunoco application form.

Make sure you fill out all areas of the application form and sign the bottom. Take the time to check over the details and edit the application if needed. You can then email the application form to a hiring manager or hand it in at your nearest Sunoco gas station.

Make an appointment…

If you are applying for a job at a Sunoco gas station, you can make an appointment to meet with the manager. This will help to speed up the hiring process, and you may even be able to work straight away. Corporate position candidates can stand out from the crowd by sending the hiring manager a personalized email.

Final Thoughts

The overall interview process depends on the type of job you are applying for. However, all Sunoco employees are expected to have strong customer service and teamwork skills.

Employees should also show a passion for creativity and the ambition to take their careers to the next level. Make sure you reflect these skills and attributes when answering common interview questions. It is also a good idea to do a little background research into Sunoco before the interview.

Prepare a few questions for the interviewer that show an interest in Sunoco and advancing through the company.
