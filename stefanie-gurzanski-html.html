Stefanie Gurzanski - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

Canadian model and Instagram influencer known for her stefaniegurzanski account, where she posts photoshoots and selfies. She has amassed 2.9 million followers on the platform. She has also done modeling work with Elle, Maxim, and Vogue Ukraine.

Before Fame

She began modeling in high school. She was named playmate of the year for Playboy Mexico in 2017.

Trivia

She was on the cover of Bode Magazine in January 2022. She was featured in an article in the issue. She also shares photos of her worldly travels with her fans on Instagram.

Family Life

She was born and raised in Ontario, Canada. She is in a relationship with Omar Benchek.

Associated With

She and Dani Mathers have both been named as Playboy playmates of the year.
