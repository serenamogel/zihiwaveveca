Naoya Inoue Siblings: Meet Takuma Inoue

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, January 30, 2024

Naoya Inoue Siblings: Meet Takuma Inoue: – Naoya Inoue was born on April 10, 1993, in Zama, Kanagawa to Miho Inoue and Shingo Inoue. He’s a Japanese professional boxer

Naoya Inoue is nicknamed The Monster and is best known for his timing, punching power, body shots, and a knockout ratio of 88%. Naoya Inoue is a three-division world champion.

He previously held the WBO junior-bantamweight title from 2014 to 2018, and the WBC light-flyweight title in 2014. He holds the WBA (Super), IBF, and Ring magazine titles since 2019, the WBC title since June 2022, and the WBO title since December 2022.

As of August 2022, he is ranked as the world’s best active boxer, pound for pound, by the Transnational Boxing Rankings Board, 2nd by The Ring, the Boxing Writers Association of America and ESPN, and 7th by BoxRec.

Naoya Inoue is the only Japanese fighter in history to be ranked number 1 pound for pound by The Ring. In December 2022, Naoya Inoue stops Paul Butler to become the undisputed bantamweight champion.

Naoya Inoue Siblings: Meet Takuma Inoue

Naoya Inoue grew up together with his younger brother whose name is Takuma Inoue. He was born on December 26, 1995, in Zama, Kanagawa, Japan.

Takuma Inoue is a Japanese professional boxer. He held the WBC interim bantamweight title from 2018 to 2019 and was challenged for the full world title in 2019.
